Some info about the band

  • The band was first founded in 2010 with the name AliveSinceForever.

  • In 2014 the band was rebranded as Brockhampton which is the name still.

  • The band has a large diversity. The group consists of the lead vocalists,
    Kevin Abstract, Matt Champion, Merlyn Wood, and Dom McLennon

  • Brockhampton has lead vocalists, vocalists, producers as well as graphic designers,
    also their manager Jon Nunes.

  • Their first ever mixtape All-American Trash was released in 2016.

  • Their debut studio album, Saturation, was released on June 9, 2017,
    after they released Saturation II and after Saturation III.

  • On March 30, 2018, Brockhampton announced that
    they had signed a record deal under the label RCA Records,
    which is a very old and famous record company.
